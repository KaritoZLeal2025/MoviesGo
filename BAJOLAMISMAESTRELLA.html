THE FAULT IN OUR
STARS

The movie tells the story of Hazel Grace Lancaster, a 16-year-old girl who, after surviving thyroid cancer, attends a support group for young cancer patients. There, she meets Augustus Waters, a charming young man who has also fought his own battle with cancer. Despite their illness, they fall deeply in love while sharing special moments and reflections on life, death, and true love. Through its heartfelt dialogues and realistic approach to life and death, The Fault in Our Stars is a movie that touches the heart and shows that even in the midst of illness and pain, love can shine in a beautiful and unique way.
