About Time

The story follows Tim Lake, a young British man who, at the age of 21, discovers that he has the ability to travel through time. Although his power allows him to change events in his life, he soon realizes that he cannot alter everything and that the most important thing is to live in the present and cherish the small moments. Throughout the film, Tim uses his gift to improve his love life and solve family problems, but he learns that the most significant decisions are those that come from the heart.
