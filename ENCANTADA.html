Enchanted

The story of Giselle is a fantasy and comedy film directed by Kevin Lima, released in 2007. It follows Giselle, a young princess from an animated kingdom called Andalasia, who is magically transported to New York City due to a curse from the evil Queen Narissa. In the city, Giselle faces a world completely different from her fairy tale, but with the help of a city lawyer named Robert and his daughter Morgan, she discovers the true meaning of love and happiness while trying to find a way back home.
