Me Before You

The story follows Louisa Clark, a quirky and outgoing 26-year-old who, after losing her job at a local café, accepts a position as a caregiver for Will Traynor, a successful businessman left quadriplegic after an accident. Throughout the film, Louisa tries to show Will that life still has meaningful aspects, while both develop a deep connection that will change their lives forever.
