Madagascar

An American animated film produced by DreamWorks Animation and directed by Eric Darnell and Tom McGrath. The voice cast includes Ben Stiller (Zoolander), Chris Rock (Down to Earth), David Schwimmer (Friends), and Jada Pinkett Smith (The Nutty Professor), among others. The film tells the story of four fun-loving animals from the Central Park Zoo in New York: Alex the lion, Marty the zebra, Gloria the hippopotamus, and Melman the giraffe. They have spent their entire lives in captivity without experiencing true freedom and come up with a plan to escape together.
