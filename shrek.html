Shrek

A long time ago, in a faraway swamp, there lived a fierce ogre named Shrek. Suddenly, one day, his solitude is interrupted by an invasion of unexpected characters. There are blind mice in his food, a big bad wolf in his bed, three homeless little pigs, and other creatures who have been banished from their land by the evil Lord Farquaad. To reclaim his home, Shrek makes a deal with Farquaad and embarks on a journey to convince the beautiful Princess Fiona to marry the Lord. On this important mission, he is accompanied by a hilarious donkey, willing to do anything for Shrek—except stay quiet.
